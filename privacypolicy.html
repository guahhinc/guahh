Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

Guahh ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website, the Coachbuddy mobile/web application, and the Coachboard administrative dashboard (collectively, the "Services"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and the players whose data is managed within our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide, maintain, and improve our Services.

A. Account Information (Coaches and Club Administrators)

To create and manage an account, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Account password (encrypted hashes)
  • Club or organisation affiliation

B. Player Data (Uploaded by Users)

Coaches and club administrators upload data regarding players to utilise the tracking and substitution features of Coachbuddy. This information is stored securely in our database systems built on Google Sheets and Google Apps Script, and may include:

  • Player Name and Jersey Number
  • Physical attributes (e.g., height)
  • Performance metrics (e.g., points scored, skills evaluations, time on court)

C. Payment Information

All subscription payments are processed securely by our third-party payment provider, Stripe. Guahh does not collect or store credit card details or bank account information on our servers. Stripe collects and processes your financial data according to their own privacy policies.

2. Protection of Minors

Because Coachbuddy is used by youth sports teams and schools, the platform frequently hosts data belonging to players under the age of 18, and accounts may be created by minor coaches (ages 13 to 16).

Consent: Club administrators and coaches are solely responsible for ensuring they have obtained necessary parental or legal guardian consent before uploading personal information of minor players into Coachbuddy.

Direct Collection: We do not knowingly or directly solicit personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If we discover that personal information of a child under 13 has been collected without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that data as quickly as possible.

3. How We Use Personal Information

We use the collected information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your login and manage your account.
  • To operate and calculate user-requested features, such as court-time tracking and automated substitution suggestions.
  • To process subscription statuses via Stripe.
  • To communicate technical updates, system alerts, or respond to support requests.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical bugs, hacking attempts, or security vulnerabilities within our Google Sheets and Apps Script infrastructure.

4. Data Sharing, Access, and Isolation Boundaries

We enforce strict technical boundaries to ensure data privacy:

  • Data Isolation: Individual coaches can only access data for their specific assigned teams. Club administrators utilising the Coachboard dashboard can only access data for teams registered under their specific club entity.
  • No Third-Party Sale: We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information or player metrics with advertising networks, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.
  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted third-party cloud infrastructure providers (Google Workspace hosting, Google Sheets, and Google Apps Script) and payment systems (Stripe) solely to assist us in operating our software, subject to strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request under Australian law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored and processed utilising Google Workspace infrastructure (including Google Sheets and Google Apps Script). We implement industry-standard administrative and technical access controls to safeguard data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of electronic transmission or digital storage is completely secure. While we work to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Accessing and Correcting Your Information

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you or to request that it be corrected or updated.

Coaches and administrators can view, edit, or delete player information directly through the Coachbuddy and Coachboard interfaces at any time.

To request complete account deletion or a copy of your stored data, contact us at the email address provided below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy, or through an in-app notice or email notification.

8. Contact Us and Complaints

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to exercise your data rights, or have a complaint regarding how we handle your privacy under the Australian Privacy Principles, please contact us at:
