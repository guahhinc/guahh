Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Betterblox Launcher ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our launcher.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the "Sign in with Roblox" feature, we receive the following information via the Roblox Open Cloud API:

  • Public Profile Information: Your Roblox username and profile avatar.
  • Unique Identifier: A unique ID provided by Roblox to verify your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Display your profile within the launcher.
  • Enable the account-switching functionality.
  • Personalize your user experience (e.g., greetings).

3. Data Storage

Betterblox Launcher is a client-side application. We do not own or maintain any external servers to store your personal data.

  • All account information, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device using your browser's Local Storage.
  • We do not have access to your Roblox passwords or private account details.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the official Roblox OAuth2 API for authentication. Your interactions with Roblox are governed by the Roblox Privacy Policy.

5. Security

By storing data only on your local device, we minimize the risk of data breaches. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your own device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at taj@guahh.net.
