FALCON3 Computer Vision AI

FALCON3 is a proprietary sports-oriented computer vision framework built by Guahh. By processing raw visual coordinate streams, FALCON3 interprets live athletic events instantly, outputting spatial reports directly to the Coachbuddy analytical backend.

Sports Object Tracking

Registers court boundaries, ball pathways, and equipment vectors dynamically with sub-pixel coordinate accuracy, logging continuous speeds and flight trajectories.

Shot & Velocity Analytics

Analyzes release structures, delivering micro-measurements regarding launch velocity, elevation angles, and point-of-origin success metrics.

Localized Jersey Number Recognition

Combines high-speed optical character recognition (OCR) with continuous frame-matching to identify and register active player identities by jersey numbers, preventing identification loss during play congestion.
